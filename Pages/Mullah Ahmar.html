Mullah Ahmar, with its fragrant blend of spices and tangy tomatoes, creates a hearty stew that warms you from the inside out
  • Ingredients
    • 1 large red onion, diced
    • 500g ground beef
    • 5tbs tomato paaste
    • 1kg tomatoes blended
    • 2tbs ground coriander
    • 1tbs ground cumin
    • 2tsp ground cinnamon
    • 4tbs ground okra
    • 5 crushed garlic cloves
  • Directions
    1. Caramelise the diced onion and garlic, then mash into a paste using a pestle and mortar and set aside
    2. Add the ground beef and spices, and cook until half way cooked
    3. Add in the tomato paste and blended tomatoes, as well as the onion paste
    4. Bring to a gentle boil, then simmer gently until the oil seperates from the stew, then stir in the okra powder. Serve with kisra or beladi
