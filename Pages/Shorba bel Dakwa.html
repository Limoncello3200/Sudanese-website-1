My personal favourite soup, Shorba bel Dakwa is packed with unmistakeably Sudanese flavours, boasting a creaminess very few soups achieve, as well as characteristicly warming and comforting spices such as cardamom and cumin.
  • Ingredients
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 4 bay leaves
    • 1tsp nutmeg
    • 2 tsp peppercorns
    • 1 onion, choped into strips
    • 10tbs crunchy peanut butter
    • 100ml milk
    • 1 lime
    • 1.5kg lamb on the bone, hacked into pieces
  • Directions
    1. Boil the lamb, onion, and spices and boil for two to three hours
    2. Stir in the peanut butter, milk, and lime, and serve
