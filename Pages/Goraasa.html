Goraasa, a pillowy, soft flatbread, is the ultimate comfort food, ideal for soaking up rich sauces and making every bite feel like a warm hug.
  • Ingredients
    • 360 g wholegrain flour
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 475-700 ml lukewarm water
    • 1 tbs salt
    • 50 ml sesame oil
  • Directions
    1. Whisk the flour, baking powder, sesame oil, water, and salt together into a large bowl.
    2. Brush a nonstick skillet with oil, then lade on the batter, spreading it out into a wide circle with the back of the ladle. Let cook over low heat until the top dries out completely, about 4-5 minutes.
