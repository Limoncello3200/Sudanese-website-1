Jute, also known as Jew's mallow is like marmite, you either love it's pungent flavour or you hate the slimy texture. Packed with spices. this stew is sure to convert even the biggest hater into a lover!
  • Ingredients
    • 1 tsp bicarbonate of soda
    • 4 garlic cloves. crushed
    • 2 tbsp inely chopped dill
    • 1.5tbs ground coriander
    • 1.5tbs ground cardamom
    • 1.5tbs ground cumin
    • 2 lamb stock cubes
    • 0.5kg jute leaves, finely chopped and rinsed
    • 1 cinnamon stick
    • 4 bay leaves
    • 1tsp nutmeg
    • 2 tsp peppercorns
    • 1 onion, choped into strips
  • Directions
    1. Add the onion and garlic to a large stock pot and cook until golden and aromatic
    2. Bring 2l of water to a boil, and add the spices and bicarbonate of soda
    3. Add chopped jute leaves, and cook for 30-40 minutes
    4. Take out half of the stew and blend until smooth, then add back to the stock pot, bring to the boil again and serve with kisra or rice.
