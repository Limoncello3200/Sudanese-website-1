A warming, comforting and filling lentil stew, shorbat addas, sometimes simply called addas, is teh perfect food for a winters night. Packed with warming spices, and a luxurious texture, it's sure top be a crowd pleaser!
  • Ingredients
    • 500g red lentils, rinsed
    • 3tbs ground cumin
    • 3tbs salt
    • 2 tbs ground black pepper
    • 4 tbs chilli powder
    • 2 limes, juiced
    • 4 cloves garlic
    • 100ml sesame oil
  • Directions
    1. Place the rinsed red lentils in a large pot. Add boiling water to cover the lentils by approx. 2 inches and bring to the boil.
    2. Reduce the heat to a gentle simmer and cover for 30-40min. Occasionally stir the base of the pan to stop the lentils sticking there.
    3. blend until smooth then season with ground cumin, salt and black pepper. If the consistency of the soup is too thick, add more boiling water.
    4. [Fry crushed garlic cloves in 100ml of hot oil and stir gently while the garlic sizzles and begins to golden. Pour the whole mixture directly into the soup and mix in evenly.
    5. Squeese the lime into the soup, and serve
